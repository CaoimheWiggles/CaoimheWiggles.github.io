Frequently Asked Questions

Will I be stuck in a contract when I join?
No! We have multiple options for you to choose from. If you wish to pay each month as you go, you can!

Do you offer discounts for students?
We do! Speak to a member of staff and they will sort that out for you.

How much does it cost per session with a personal trainer?
You must contact the trainer to let them know what you're looking for and they will discuss the payment with you.

Are there showers/lockers provided in the gym?
Yes! There are multiple showers and lockers. We have locks available to purchase at reception.

Is it possible to freeze my membership temporarily if I will be away or if I've gotten injured?
Yes this is possible. You will need to let staff know as soon as you'd like to freeze your membership.
